Our Story

Rooted in Tradition,
Driven by Quality

SRIRAM TRADERS is a proprietorship firm established by P. Mala, operating a fully equipped cashew processing and manufacturing unit in Panruti Taluk, Cuddalore District — one of India's most renowned cashew-producing regions in Tamil Nadu.

From sourcing raw cashews to precision processing, grading, and packaging, every step is managed in-house to ensure consistent quality. We supply directly to bulk buyers, wholesalers, supermarkets, export houses, and individual traders across India.

Every cashew that leaves our facility carries the promise of purity, freshness, and the warmth of Tamil Nadu's finest produce.
